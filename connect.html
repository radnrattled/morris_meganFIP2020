Hey there I'm

After graduating Western I took off to the west coast to write poetry in the mountains. After more adventure I joined a travelling circus and travelled all over North America for a year. It was during this time in my life I fell in love with places, people and making connections. It wasn't untill I went everywhere looking for something I couldn't define that I realized I would never find my home, I had to create it. Finding the Most beautiful places in the world is less about where you are, and more about who you are.
